HARDWARE

The smart air conditioner utilizes advanced sensors and algorithms to monitor temperature and occupancy patterns. It dynamically adjusts cooling output based on real-time data, optimizing energy usage. By intelligently adapting to the environment, the air conditioner minimizes waste, reduces energy bills, and maintains optimal comfort levels throughout the day.

The smart escalator employs sensors to track foot traffic and usage patterns. It dynamically controls its operation, powering on and off intelligently based on real-time data. By adapting to actual needs rather than running continuously, the escalator minimizes energy consumption without compromising convenience for users.

The smart lighting system utilizes sensors to monitor ambient light levels and occupancy. It automatically adjusts the intensity of the lights or turns them on/off based on the available natural light and detected human presence. This adaptive functionality minimizes unnecessary lighting usage, reducing energy consumption while maintaining optimal illumination.
